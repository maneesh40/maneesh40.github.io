Kanpur (208016), UP, India

E-mail: maneeshp@iitk.ac.in

Recently joined as Senior Research Fellow on August 03, 2020, with Prof. Sameer Khandekar, Mechanical Engineering Department, IIT Kanpur

I am currently working as an Senior Research Fellow in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur with Professor Sameer Khandekar. I completed my PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur in July, 2020. I obtained my M.Tech in Thermal System Design from Sardar Vallabhbhai Natioal Institure of Technology in 2014. I belongs to a beautiful valley city, Pithoragarh, in the north east of Uttrakhand. (See Photos).

I love to be creative in my work, be it academic or extra curricular. I am passionate about doing experimental work in the field of multiphase flow and heat transfer (particularly in phase change), which is the backbone of future electronics and energy systems. In particular, I am interested to develop novel engineering solutions to meet the challenges of energy and cooling system across the world, with special focus on safety and environment.
