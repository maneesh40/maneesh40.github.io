E-mail: punetha@kth.se

I will be joining as an Assistant Professor at School of Mechanical Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar, India in December 2023  

I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden, with a background in Mechanical Engineering (Ph.D. from IIT Kanpur, India in 2020). Previously, I worked as a Senior Research Fellow at IIT Kanpur and Assistant Manager at ESSAR Steel India. I also hold Masters and Bachelors degrees in Mechanical Engineering.

I am passionate about phase change, multiphase flows and heat transfer experiments. My research focuses on developing efficient and reliable cooling systems, crucial for energy systems and electronics. At NPS Division, KTH, we work on sustainable engineering solutions in the nuclear energy sector with a focus on safety and environment.

I come from the beautiful valley city of Pithoragarh in Uttrakhand, India (See Photos). I enjoy bringing creativity to both my academic and extracurricular work.
