Started working as Postdoctoral Fellow in Nuclear Power Safety Division, Department of Physics, School of Engineering Sciences, KTH Royal Institute of Technology from June 21, 2021  

I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Fellow with Professor Bechta, Dr. Villanueva and Dr. Ma in the Department of Physics, School of Engineering Sciences, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden. In past, I worked as a Senior Research Fellow with Professor Sameer Khandekar in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India. I completed my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India in July, 2020. Before that, I obtained my M.Tech. in Thermal System Design from Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat, India in July, 2014. I am passionate about doing experimental work in the field of multiphase flow and heat transfer (particularly in phase change), which is the backbone of future electronics and energy systems. In particular, I am interested to develop imperative engineering solutions by considering the future need of our society, with special focus on safety and environment.

I belong to a beautiful valley city, Pithoragarh, in the north east of Uttrakhand, India. (See Photos). I love to be creative in my work, be it academic or extra curricular.
